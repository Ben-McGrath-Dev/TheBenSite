The House of No Eyes

NOTE: This books is subject to change, while the story will stay the same, if i get enough feedback about the writing itself I will change it. Please leave constructive criticism!

The House of No Eyes is a short horror story about a young teenage boy who ventures into a house he should've never even though of stepping into. This is because he is unaware that the house is inhabited by a strange creature called Nekromantis or The Lurker. This deep dive into the horrors and government coverups he unwilling steps into reveals secrets you will never forget.

Content warning

This book may not be suitable for younger readers or readers who do not like horror, violence, death of a child protagonist, teeth being ripped out, implied/mentioned child experimentation, brainwashing, government disposal of witnesses, and explicit references to humans being tortured or killed by the main organisation. Reader discretion is adviced.
